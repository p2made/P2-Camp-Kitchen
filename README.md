##<a id="notes"></a> *Notes*

“Front”, “Back”, “Left”, & “Right” are from the viewpoint of facing the 1600 mm side that is the kitchen front when deployed & to the front when stowed. That makes “left” & “right” opposite to car driver’s seat viewpoint.
